Time-line of Michael Jordan's illustrious NBA career

 :
  • 1984-Michael Jordan Enters the NBA Draft and Is Selected by the Chicago Bulls
  • 1985-Michael Jordan Wins Rookie of the Year
  • 1987-1988-Michael Jordan Averages 32.5 Points Per Game, Winning the MVP Award and Defensive Player of the Year
  • 1989-Michael Jordan makes a game winning shot known as "The Shot" in Game 5 of the first round of the 1989 playoffs
  • 1991-Michael Jordan wins his first championship against the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 1991-1998-Michael Jordan and the Chicago Bulls win 6 championships during in 8 years
 :

With his 6 championships, Michael Jordan has also won numerous awards, including

  1. Rookie of the Year
  2. NBA's Most Valuable Player (x5)
  3. NBA's season scoring title (x10)
  4. Olympic Gold Medal (x2)
  5. And Most importantly, the title as the greatest basketball player of all time
